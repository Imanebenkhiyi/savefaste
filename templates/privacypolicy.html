Privacy Policy

At SaveFaste, protecting user privacy is one of our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when users access our website and online PDF tools.

Last Updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

When users interact with SaveFaste, certain information may be collected to improve services and ensure platform functionality.

  • Personal information such as name or email address when contacting support or creating an account.
  • Usage information including visited pages, browser type, device information, and session activity.
  • Technical analytics collected through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Information

The collected information helps us operate and improve our services while providing a better user experience.

  • Maintain and improve PDF tools and website functionality.
  • Respond to support requests and user inquiries.
  • Monitor website performance and usage statistics.
  • Improve security, stability, and service reliability.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SaveFaste uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic.

  • Remember user preferences and settings.
  • Understand how visitors use the website.
  • Improve website performance and personalization.
  • Support advertising and analytics services where applicable.

Users can manage cookie settings directly through their browsers. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.

4. Sharing Information

SaveFaste does not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

Information may only be shared with trusted service providers who assist in operating the website, maintaining infrastructure, or delivering services under confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational security measures are implemented to protect personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

Uploaded files processed through PDF tools are handled securely and may be automatically deleted after processing.

6. User Rights

Depending on local laws and regulations, users may have rights related to their personal information.

  • Request access to stored information.
  • Request correction or deletion of personal data.
  • Object to certain types of processing.
  • Withdraw consent where legally applicable.

To exercise any privacy-related rights, users may contact SaveFaste support directly.

7. Children's Privacy

SaveFaste services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect service improvements, legal requirements, or operational changes. Continued use of the website after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
