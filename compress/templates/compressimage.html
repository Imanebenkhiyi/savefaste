What is Image Compression?

Image compression is the process of reducing the file size of photos while keeping their quality as high as possible. This helps you save storage space, upload faster, and improve your website’s performance.

Benefits of Compressing Images

  • Smaller file size: Save storage space on your device or server.
  • Faster uploads: Compressed images upload quickly on websites and apps.
  • Better website performance: Reduce loading time and improve SEO ranking.
  • Easy sharing: Send compressed images faster via email or messaging apps.
  • Preserves quality: Keep your image looking sharp while decreasing its size.

How to Use This Tool

To compress your images, simply upload one or multiple files using the “Select Images” button or drag & drop them into the upload area. After selecting the images, click “Compress Now” to instantly reduce their file size.
